# Allergen List

Uber Eats supports **food allergies recognized by local regulatory agencies**, which include:  

- Bee pollen  
- Celery  
- Cereals with gluten  
- Crustaceans  
- Eggs  
- Fish  
- Lupin  
- Milk  
- Molluscs  
- Mustard  
- Peanuts  
- Royal jelly  
- Sesame  
- Soybeans  
- Sulphur dioxide/sulphites  
- Tree nuts  
- **Other**  

Customers may also make requests regarding allergens **not on the list**.
